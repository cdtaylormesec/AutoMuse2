Two ghosts that were once intimately connected in life are now standing coldly and shuddering from each other in mutual dread, because they are not yet used to being disembodied beings. They are also both scared of themselves, because their consciousness has been revealed to them in a way that life never does, except at such intense moments. They slowly and hesitantly reach out to touch each other's hand, and then they sit down and start talking about things that are bothering them the most.
