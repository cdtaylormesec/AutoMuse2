I sincerely hope your Christmas in Hertfordshire may       abound in the gaieties which that season generally brings, and       that your beaux will be so numerous as to prevent your feeling       the loss of the three of whom we shall deprive you.”        “It is evident by this,” added Jane, “that he comes back no more       this winter.”        “It is only evident that Miss Bingley does not mean that he       _should_.”        “Why will you think so?
