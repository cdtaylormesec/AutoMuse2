She expressed her gratitude again, but it was too painful a       subject to each, to be dwelt on farther.        After walking several miles in a leisurely manner, and too busy       to know anything about it, they found at last, on examining their       watches, that it was time to be at home.        “What could become of Mr. Bingley and Jane!” was a wonder which       introduced the discussion of _their_ affairs. Darcy was delighted       with their engagement; his friend had given him the earliest       information of it.        “I must ask whether you were surprised?” said Elizabeth.        “Not at all. When I went away, I felt that it would soon happen.”        “That is to say, you had given your permission.
