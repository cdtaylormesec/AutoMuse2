The pale Usher—threadbare in coat, heart, body, and brain; I see him   now. He was ever dusting his old lexicons and grammars, with a queer   handkerchief, mockingly embellished with all the gay flags of all the   known nations of the world. He loved to dust his old grammars; it   somehow mildly reminded him of his mortality.    “While you take in hand to school others, and to teach them by what   name a whale-fish is to be called in our tongue, leaving out, through   ignorance, the letter H, which almost alone maketh up the   signification of the word, you deliver that which is not true.”   —_Hackluyt._    “WHALE. * * * Sw. and Dan. _hval_.
