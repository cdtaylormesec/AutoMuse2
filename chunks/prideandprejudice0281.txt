How much I shall have to tell!”        Elizabeth added privately, “And how much I shall have to       conceal!”        Their journey was performed without much conversation, or any       alarm; and within four hours of their leaving Hunsford they       reached Mr. Gardiner’s house, where they were to remain a few       days.        Jane looked well, and Elizabeth had little opportunity of       studying her spirits, amidst the various engagements which the       kindness of her aunt had reserved for them. But Jane was to go       home with her, and at Longbourn there would be leisure enough for       observation.
