Forgive me for having taken up so much of your time, and       accept my best wishes for your health and happiness.”        And with these words he hastily left the room, and Elizabeth       heard him the next moment open the front door and quit the house.        The tumult of her mind, was now painfully great. She knew not how       to support herself, and from actual weakness sat down and cried       for half-an-hour. Her astonishment, as she reflected on what had       passed, was increased by every review of it. That she should       receive an offer of marriage from Mr. Darcy! That he should have       been in love with her for so many months! So much in love as to       wish to marry her in spite of all the objections which had made       him prevent his friend’s marrying her sister, and which must       appear at least with equal force in his own case—was almost       incredible! It was gratifying to have inspired unconsciously so       strong an affection.
