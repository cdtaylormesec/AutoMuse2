Would that I could clear out Hampton Court and the   Tuileries for ye! But gulp down your tears and hie aloft to the   royal-mast with your hearts; for your friends who have gone before   are clearing out the seven-storied heavens, and making refugees of   long-pampered Gabriel, Michael, and Raphael, against your coming.   Here ye strike but splintered hearts together—there, ye shall strike   unsplinterable glasses!
