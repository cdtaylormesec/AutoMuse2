You were disgusted with the women who       were always speaking, and looking, and thinking for _your_       approbation alone. I roused, and interested you, because I was so       unlike _them_. Had you not been really amiable, you would have       hated me for it; but in spite of the pains you took to disguise       yourself, your feelings were always noble and just; and in your       heart, you thoroughly despised the persons who so assiduously       courted you. There—I have saved you the trouble of accounting for       it; and really, all things considered, I begin to think it       perfectly reasonable. To be sure, you knew no actual good of       me—but nobody thinks of _that_ when they fall in love.”        “Was there no good in your affectionate behaviour to Jane while       she was ill at Netherfield?”        “Dearest Jane! who could have done less for her?
